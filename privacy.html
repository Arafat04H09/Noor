Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2024

Noor ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information.

1. Data Minimization

We are "Private by Default". We do not sell your personal data. We do not track your location. We use iCloud (CloudKit) to sync your data, meaning your private reflections are stored in Apple's secure cloud infrastructure.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Info: Name and Email (via Sign in with Apple) for account management.
  • User Content: Text, Audio, and Images you upload as reflections.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics to improve app stability (crashes).

3. How We Use Information

We use your information solely to provide the App's functionality: syncing your journal with your partner and maintaining your reading history.

4. Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your account at any time. You can do this directly within the app: Settings > Account > Sign Out & Delete.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions, please contact us at: salam@trynoor.xyz
